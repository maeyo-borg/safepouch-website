How a show runs

Guests keep their phones the whole night — just locked while the music's on.

1. Pouch up at the door

Guests drop their phone into a SafePouch as they enter and keep it on them — no bag check, no surrendering devices.

2. Present for the whole set

Need a quick text? Step to an unlock station, then re-lock and head back in. The floor stays screen-free.

3. Unlock on the way out

Tap to unlock at the exits as the room empties — our on-site lead handles the flow so your staff don't have to.

Concrete outcome

A crowd that actually shows up for the show, without venue staff playing camera police.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

SafePouch ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains, in plain English, what information we receive through this website and how we use it. By using this site, you agree to this policy.

The short version

We don't sell your information, we don't run ads, and we don't track you across the web. The only personal information we get is what you choose to send us when you contact us — your name, email, and message — and we use it only to reply.

Information we collect

  • What you send us. Our contact form opens your own email app and sends a message to us. When you contact us, we receive your name, email address, organization, and any details you include (such as size, timing, or your message).
  • Basic server logs. Like most websites, our web host may automatically keep standard technical logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability. We do not use these to identify you.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide pricing, specs, or a pilot plan.
  • To communicate with you about SafePouch and your request.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve this website.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (for example, email delivery), or when required by law.

Data retention

We keep contact information only as long as needed to respond to your request and for our legitimate business records, unless you ask us to delete it sooner.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing safepouchco@gmail.com.

Cookies & tracking

This site does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. If we ever add analytics, we will update this policy to explain what is collected and how to opt out.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email safepouchco@gmail.com.
